About

I didn't set out to be a finance person. 我從來不是「財務人」出身。

I've always been fascinated by how businesses really work — the messy decisions made with incomplete information, and why smart people so often make predictably bad financial calls.

That curiosity led me through finance, operations, and now AI. Along the way, I've pulled together decades of real-world experience and mentorship into something sharper. I started Fanance.ai to share the condensed version: counterintuitive insights that actually matter.

No hype, no fluff — just my two cents, straight from the trenches. Financial clarity, practical AI workflows, and the mental models that quietly run companies.

我一直著迷於企業真實運轉的底層邏輯——在資訊不完整的情況下如何做決策，以及為什麼聰明人會反覆犯下那些可預測的財務錯誤。

這份好奇心帶領我走過金融、營運，現在進入AI領域。這些年，我把數十年的實戰經驗和導師指點濃縮提煉。我創立 Fanance.ai，就是為了分享這些經過濃縮的洞見：那些真正有價值的、反直覺的思考。

沒有炒作，沒有廢話——只有來自實戰壕溝的直接觀點。財務清晰度、實用的AI工作流程，以及那些默默主導企業運營的心智模型。

What I write about

Three things I think about constantly. 三個我持續深思的主題。

01

Financial Clarity

Most financial confusion isn't about math — it's about mental models. I break down how to actually read a business, understand cash flow, and make decisions under uncertainty. 大多數財務困惑不在於數學——而在於心智模型。我拆解如何真正讀懂一門生意、理解現金流，並在不確定性中做出決策。

02

AI × Ops

AI doesn't replace thinking. It amplifies it. I explore where AI tools genuinely change how operators work — and where they just add noise. AI不會取代思考，它會放大思考。我探索AI工具真正改變營運方式的地方——以及它只是製造噪音的地方。

03

Founder Decisions

The hardest calls founders face are usually financial. I write about pricing, burn, hiring leverage, and the moments that define a company's trajectory. 創辦人面臨的最難抉擇通常是財務性的。我寫定價、燒錢率、招募槓桿，以及那些定義公司軌跡的關鍵時刻。
