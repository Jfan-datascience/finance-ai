About

The CFO who chose to talk about it.

I started my career chasing a title. At 24, I became a CFO at a startup — and it nearly broke me. What they don't tell you: the sleepless board meetings, the fundraising rejections, the loneliness of being the only finance person in a room full of dreamers.

I went on to work across 6 industries — Intel, AMD, Chowbus, Kandi, Outdoorsy — before finding my footing. Along the way I helped raise $81M in a Series E, built FP&A functions from scratch, and ended up on an AFP panel talking about it.

Now I create content under the 空中MBA (Air MBA) brand — helping finance professionals and founders learn what school skips. 400K+ views, 31 countries, and still going.
